The beginning

In late 2020, I decided to purchase a drone. Chose the Mavic Air 2 to start visiting some of the drainageways my work had project along.

Software

The vast majority of the photos and video were collected using the native DJI Fly App. Videos were created using Adobe Premiere Pro. Planning on using the Pix4D suite of software when collecting photogrametry data.
