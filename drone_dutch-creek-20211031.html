Dutch Creek at Coal Mine Road

October 31, 2021
Drone Dutch Creek

Project Background

Dutch Creek is a left bank tributary to the South Platte River. This project, designed by ICON Engineering, replaced two severely undersized corrugated metal pipe culverts with a triple cell reinforced box culvert. The new crossing will prevent flows from spilling onto Coal Mine Road, allowing traffic to continue even in large rain events.

Drone Flight

This was one of the most exciting / scary flights to date. You can see in the video below the sensors on the drone stopped the flight several times at the entrance and exit of the box culvert.
